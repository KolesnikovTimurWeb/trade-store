PRIVACY POLICY

INTRODUCTION

This Privacy Policy (hereinafter referred to as the "Policy") applies to the Company and regulates the collection, storage, use, and disclosure of personal information about you, whether you are a User, a Client, or a potential customer. It also applies to visitors of the Company's websites. We guarantee the confidentiality of all personal data received, including information obtained during your visit to our website

COLLECTION OF PERSONAL DATA

The company Tradingbrokerpicker complies with applicable legal regulations when using users' personal data. The company is registered as a Data Controller with the Office of the Commissioner for Personal Data Protection in accordance with Directive 2016/680 of the European Union on the processing of personal data and Regulation (EU) 2016/679 on the protection of natural persons with regard to the processing of personal data and on the free movement of such data. Personal data will be processed by the Company in accordance with the Privacy Policy, Cookie Policy, and Trading Terms of the Company. The company collects personal data and establishes the purposes, conditions, and methods of their processing, which makes it a data controller. A data processor is a natural or legal person who processes personal data on behalf of the controller. When filling out an online application for a real or demo account, forms of another type, or using any other service that the Company provides through its website, the Company will collect and store personal data about the user. When filling out any application or requested form, the user provides personal information necessary to evaluate the request/application and comply with laws and regulations governing the provision of Forex/CFD services and/or any other financial instruments. This information may also be used to communicate with the user regarding the Company's services if they have consented to this during the registration process. The company conducts a registration process on its website to collect documents verifying the identity of potential clients in accordance with the company's internal policies and procedures. This allows the company to assess the possibility of business relationships with the potential client. The company collects personal data of each potential client and uses it only for evaluation and storage in electronic or physical form on the company's disk, server, and files in its premises. Such data is processed only for collection purposes, such as determining potential business relationships, marketing, evaluating services provided by the company, and other purposes related to the aforementioned reasons. The company collects certain personal data to meet the needs of customers through the efficient provision of services. This also allows the company to monitor and improve services for its current and future clients. The company collects personal data, including but not limited to the following categories:

1) Personal data, such as name, surname, phone number, email address, and education;

2) Financial data, including expected annual income, own capital, trading experience, and investment knowledge;

3) Identity documents, such as a passport, ID card, utility bills, bank statements, copies of cards or company registration certificates, and other corporate documents

The company may request additional information if it deems it necessary depending on the circumstances of each case The company has the right to obtain information about the user's use of the website (such as cookie files) and store this information in a personal profile. This information may include the sections of the website visited, pages viewed by the user, as well as the frequency and duration of visits, types of transactions conducted, documents downloaded, and other websites the user has visited. It should also be noted that the company may process personal data if necessary in accordance with applicable laws and regulations to which it is subject

USE OF PERSONAL DATA

The purposes for using a user's personal data by a company may include the following:

11) Transferring the user's personal data to third parties for marketing purposes in accordance with the user's consent provided at registration
