DEPOSIT & TRANSFER

DEPOSITS

To deposit funds, it is necessary to specify their source - this can be a bank account, online payments, or a credit/debit card. In order to avoid payments from unauthorized individuals, the client must confirm their ownership of the card or account. If the client wants to deposit funds using a card, they must provide a scanned copy or photo of the card, as well as undergo verification according to the AML policy

It is important to follow this rule, otherwise the deposit may be rejected. It should be noted that due to fees, the amount credited to the Tradingbrokerpicker account may differ from the amount initially sent

The company does not charge commission fees, however, banks or online payment systems may do so. More information can be found on the deposit and withdrawal page

WITHDRAWALS

The client should use the same bank account, credit/debit card, or payment system account that was used to deposit funds and withdraw money in the same currency in accordance with the POC rules and regulations

Additional fees and restrictions may apply when withdrawing or depositing funds for trading purposes. Tradingbrokerpicker may allow funds to be withdrawn to another account if it complies with anti-money laundering laws without violating the aforementioned rules

Additional information and documentation may be required from the client during the withdrawal process

WITHDRAWING
PROCESS

WITHDRAWING PROCESS

1. Log in to your personal account and authenticate yourself

2. Withdraw funds from your trading account and transfer them to MoneyBox using the "Internal Transfer" button

3. After the internal transfer has been processed, submit a request to withdraw money from MoneyBox to a bank account or credit card

4. Fill in the fields on the "Withdrawal" tab If we require additional documents, we will contact you on the next business day. If there are two or more active requisites, the withdrawal will be made proportionally to the contributions on each one. Please note that money can only be withdrawn from bank accounts after a corresponding bank deposit has been made. We do not charge any withdrawal fees, but the recipient's bank may charge a commission. Once we receive a client's request to withdraw funds, we will complete the withdrawal process within 5 business days

Attention! In accordance with international laws to prevent financing of terrorism and combat money laundering, Tradingbrokerpicker does not accept payments from third parties and does not make payments to third parties. The company is not responsible for delays caused by actions of third parties. Usually, bank transfers take 3 to 5 business days. Within 1-2 business days of a withdrawal request, Tradingbrokerpicker processes withdrawals to Visa, Mastercard, and China Union Pay cards. However, please note that payment processing may take up to 6 banking days from the time the withdrawal request is received

What is a underlying asset?

An underlying asset is any other financial asset that is used as the basis for an option. This financial asset can be represented by a commodity, index, stock, currency pair, or other financial asset

How are prices displayed on the trading platform?

On the trading platform, prices that are rising are displayed in green, and prices that are falling are displayed in red

What time is indicated on this website?

The time on this website is synchronized with the market time of the country in which the user is located.
