DEPOSIT & WITHDRAWALS

DEPOSIT AND WITHDRAWALS POLICY (WD POLICY)

To take advantage of any investment opportunity that can generate income, Tradingbrokerpicker Trading clients must deposit funds into their online account. If there is profit, it can be withdrawn from the account at any time. Deposit and withdrawal operations are controlled by the WD policy as well as the general terms and conditions

DEPOSITS

To make deposits into your trading account, as a client, you must use only one source (for example, a single bank account). Prior to trading, it is necessary to ensure that the trading account is registered in your name and in your country of residence. To confirm the origin of the funds used for trading, authentic SWIFT confirmation must be provided to Tradingbrokerpicker Trading. Failure to comply with the WD policy may result in a ban on depositing funds via bank or wire transfers. If you have not logged in and traded from your account for a period of three (3) months, monthly fees of 10% will be deducted from your account

USE OF PERSONAL DATA

2) Creating and maintaining the user's profile;

3) Assessing the suitability and appropriateness of the products and services being offered;

4) Providing the services requested by the user, including processing transactions;

5) Providing additional services related to the transaction and its subsequent stages;

6) Informing the user about products and services that may be of interest to them;

7) Keeping the user informed about issues related to their business relationship with the company;

8) Adapting the website to the user's needs and interests;

9) Analyzing anonymous statistical data to improve the quality of the products and services provided by the company;

10) Administering the user's account, monitoring their behavior, and evaluating and analyzing their credit limit, including interest rates, fees, and other expenses related to the trading account;

11) Transferring the user's personal data to third parties for marketing purposes in accordance with the

user's consent provided at registration

WITHDRAWALS

The withdrawal of funds must be made to the same bank account or credit/debit card that was used for the deposit, in accordance with generally accepted rules and regulations. Unless otherwise agreed beforehand, withdrawals from the Account can only be made in the same currency as the corresponding deposit. When using alternative payment methods for depositing or withdrawing funds for trading purposes, the Client must take into account possible additional commissions and restrictions. A processing and withdrawal fee will be charged for withdrawals, which will be deducted from the withdrawn amount. The commission schedule is available on Tradingbrokerpicker Trading. Tradingbrokerpicker Trading may withdraw funds to a different instrument than the one used for deposit if it complies with anti-money laundering rules. In addition, the client may be asked to provide additional information and documents when withdrawing funds. Please note that if the above rules and regulations are followed, fund withdrawals occur smoothly and without delay
